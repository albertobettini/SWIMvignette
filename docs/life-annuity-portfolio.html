SWIM: Scenario Weights for Importance Measurement

5 Life Annuity Portfolio

In this section we showcase the SWIM package to conduct sensitivity analysis of a life annuity portfolio.

Model inspired by Olivieri & Pitacco (2003). See also Hari et al. (2009) for an application to a pension scheme. Point of view of an insurer who aims at building up a partial internal model for a portfolio of life annuities.

Types of risks:
+ The mortality risk is originated by the random lifetimes of annuitants. It can be split in process and longevity risk.
- The investment risk originates from the randomness stemming from financial markets in which the insurer invests.
